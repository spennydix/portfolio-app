The Process

I want to say that I always start by talking to people with the problem we are trying to solve… but that just isn’t the case. Most of the time, a company is coming to us because we offer tools to solve common problems they are having.

We typically start by talking to a brand, about their customers, about their products.

Then we talk to users, experts, and haters.

Collaboration

After talking to people, the brand and internal stakeholders, I meet with a small project team, in a brainstorm meeting, where we wireframe, storyboard, and get a loose script for the story we are going to tell, the interactions we are going to build and the information we are going to try to get people to digest and remember.

Deliverables from this meeting are disparate, but as long as we leave with a plan, we have succeeded.
